Who is Fatih UYSAL?

Fatih UYSAL is a versatile security researcher who embraces the "Purple Team" doctrine, merging the creative strategies of an attacker with the analytical discipline of a defender. His primary mission is to build proactive defense lines by identifying vulnerabilities in complex digital ecosystems before they can be exploited, and to strengthen security operations through automation. For him, cybersecurity is not just a field of technical expertise, but a passion built on continuous learning and discovery, aimed at making the digital world safer while remaining committed to ethical values.
